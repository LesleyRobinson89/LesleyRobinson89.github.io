Sexy Succulents

Fleshy gems of the plant world

Succulent plants are a common feature of most plant-lovers' gardens and homes. Not only do they resemble weird, bulbous alien life-forms, they can also be hideously neglected and still live to tell the tale. They also come in a huge variety - one even looks like a brain.

It is said that "the dessert works constantly to forbid it and still the cactus blooms." This quote demonstrates the tenacity of the succulent plant and its determination to thrive in most environments.

Propogation

One can have great fun propogating one plant into many specimens, like a greedy green-fingered god.

Steps for propogating your succulents:

  1. Cut a green piece of an existing succulent beneath the node
  2. Stick this cutting into a container of moist soil
  3. Water the cutting
  4. Place the container into moderate sun

Keeping your succulents alive

Here are some useful tips for keeping your succulents alive and well:

  • Go easy on the water
  • Make sure their containers have drainage holes
  • Get the light right
  • Plant in well-drained soil
